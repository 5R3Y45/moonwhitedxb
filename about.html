Our Journey

Moonwhite General Trading LLC is a dynamic and fast-growing general trading company based in the United Arab Emirates, specializing primarily in the wholesale distribution of premium apparel across the region. Established in 2019, we have become a trusted supply partner to major hypermarket networks and retail groups, delivering quality, style, and value.

With our head office strategically located in Deira, Dubai, and a dedicated distribution hub in Industrial Area 18, Muveilah, Sharjah, we operate with efficiency and speed. Our in-house logistics fleet ensures same-day delivery across all seven Emirates, supporting the continuous supply needs of our clients.

We proudly serve leading retail groups including Mark-n-Save (Subsidiary of Nesto Group), Al Madina Group, Big Market, Talal Group, Shaklan Group, Al Madina logistics, EMG general trading, PA sons group and Regency Grand Group, making us a strong player in the competitive apparel distribution sector.

Our Partners

Mark & Save Hypermarket

As a premium subsidiary of Western Group of companies, they are the fastest pacing brand in the retail sector across the Middle East.

Talal Group

Since 1983, Talal Group has built an exemplary network of retail and wholesale distribution channels and is a trusted entity in the UAE.

Al Madina Distribution

Started in the 1970s, Al Madina has a strong retail presence with over 100 outlets. Headquartered in Dubai, they also operate jewelry stores and other businesses.

Regency Grand Group

Headquartered in Dubai and established in 1994, Regency Grand employs over 6000 people across multiple countries including the UAE, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, China, and India.

Shaklan Group

Founded in 1997 in Dubai, Shaklan Group has diverse interests in consumer retail, real estate, and restaurants.

EMG Group Abu Dhabi

Part of Emirates Madina Group, EMG is known for its focus on customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and wide product range.

Pasons Group

Established in 1986, Pasons Group is a major player in retail stores, restaurants, and electronic products.

Al Madina Logistics

Founded in 1971, Al Madina is a trusted name among the Indian diaspora in the UAE, popular for daily essentials and more.

Al Madina – Port Saeed

Located in Port Saeed, Deira, Dubai, this branch offers fresh produce, electronics, and household essentials. Open daily from 7:00 AM to 1:00 AM.
