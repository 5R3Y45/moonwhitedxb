Who We Are

Moon White General Trading LLC is a Dubai-based firm with nearly a decade of experience in the men’s apparel industry. With sourcing channels across India, China, Indonesia, Taiwan, and Turkey, and operations managed from Sharjah, we operate as exporters, wholesalers, and retailers serving the Middle East and North Africa. Our subsidiaries - La Mirabel and Manever - further expand our portfolio to meet diverse retail demands

Our Partners

Mark & Save Hypermarket

As a premium subsidiary of Western Group of companies, they are the fastest pacing brand in the retail sector across the Middle East.

Talal Group

Since 1983, Talal Group has built an exemplary network of retail and wholesale distribution channels and is a trusted entity in the UAE.

Al Madina Distribution

Started in the 1970s, Al Madina has a strong retail presence with over 100 outlets. Headquartered in Dubai, they also operate jewelry stores and other businesses.

Regency Grand Group

Headquartered in Dubai and established in 1994, Regency Grand employs over 6000 people across multiple countries including the UAE, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, China, and India.

Shaklan Group

Founded in 1997 in Dubai, Shaklan Group has diverse interests in consumer retail, real estate, and restaurants.

EMG Group Abu Dhabi

Part of Emirates Madina Group, EMG is known for its focus on customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and wide product range.

Pasons Group

Established in 1986, Pasons Group is a major player in retail stores, restaurants, and electronic products.

AIKO Hypermarket

AIKO Hypermarket, part of the Abreco Group, is a one-stop destination for daily essentials and value shopping in the UAE.

Al Madina Logistics

Back from 1971, Al Madina is a trusted brand name inside Indian diaspora in UAE. Popular among working class customers for all their daily needs.
